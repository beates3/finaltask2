Article 1

The animal sketches of Picasso tended to be simple, abstract and upbeat. Cat and Bird is a moody oil painting which captured the stealth like skills of our feline friends.Early on in his career, Picasso noticed the observational skills of cats. They would always be alert and aware of what ever was going on close by. An early pencil sketch in 1902 from his time in Barcelona captured a cat watching on as a nude couple lied beside each other.
